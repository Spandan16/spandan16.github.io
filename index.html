Projects

Title: Automatic School Bell   (Patented)

Skill Used: Arduino

This bell is microcontroller-based which works on Network Time Protocol. This NTP is used in Railway, Airlines, mobile network and many important places to maintain accurecy of time. So it will never be late or fast for a single second. This bell is set as per the routine of any school, that works only for School days and school hours. On Sunday it will not work. If the time of routine is different for different days it will work accordingly.

Title: Milk Pasteurization Device   (Patented)

Skill Used: Arduino

Pasteurization is a process of rising up the temperature of Milk to 71.7 degree centigrade for 15 seconds to 25 seconds and then cool-down quickly below 3 degree centigrade. But the electronic devices we have like micro-wave oven and induction cooker do not measure the actual temperature of milk and so it is not possible to maintain exact temperature of milk. It is a special heater fitted with temperature sensor which will raise the temperature of milk to 71.7 degree centigrade and then starts counting time for 21 seconds. After completion of time it gives me a Buzzer to put the milk in Deep fridge.
